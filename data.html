System Archetypes

Success to the Successful: Platforms reward already successful sellers, making it harder for new or small sellers to compete. Limits to Growth: Small sellers initially grow but plateau due to rising costs and low visibility. Shifting the Burden: Platforms rely on paid promotions instead of solving root issues like fair access and seller support.
