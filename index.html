About FairMarket

FairMarket India is a research initiative focused on understanding and addressing the systemic challenges that small sellers face in the rapidly growing e-commerce landscape of India.

Through systems thinking and data-driven analysis, we aim to create a more equitable marketplace where small businesses can thrive alongside larger players.
