Understanding the Problem Space

Small sellers on e-commerce platforms face a complex set of challenges that limit their growth potential and sustainability. Our research identifies key systemic issues that create barriers to success.

Visibility Competition Algorithms Pricing Policy

Key Challenges Identified

Our research has identified these major challenges facing small sellers in the e-commerce ecosystem

All Problems Competition Pricing Platform Policies

Visibility Challenges

Small sellers struggle to get their products discovered among millions of listings on major platforms.

Only 5% of products appear on the first page of search results

Algorithm Bias

Platform algorithms often favor established sellers with higher sales volumes and better metrics.

New sellers see 40% less traffic than established ones

Pricing Pressure

Intense competition forces small sellers to reduce prices, often below sustainable margins.

Average profit margins have decreased by 15% in the last 3 years

Platform Dependency

Sellers become dependent on platforms, with little control over policy changes that affect their business.

78% of small sellers rely on a single platform for over 80% of sales

Counterfeit Products

Small brands face challenges from counterfeit products that undercut prices and damage reputation.

Estimated ₹40,000 Crore annual loss due to counterfeits

Data Asymmetry

Limited access to market data compared to platforms and larger sellers creates competitive disadvantages.

Large sellers have access to 5x more market data points
